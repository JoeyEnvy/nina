Frequently Asked Questions

Your inquiries, expertly answered

What are your opening hours?

We are open Monday to Friday from 11:00 AM to 11:00 PM, and on weekends from 10:00 AM to midnight.

Do you offer vegetarian options?

Yes, our menu includes a variety of vegetarian and vegan dishes crafted with fresh, local ingredients.

Can I book a table online?

Absolutely, you can make reservations directly through our website or by calling us at any time.

Is parking available?

Yes, we offer complimentary valet parking for all our guests.

Do you accommodate large group reservations?

Yes, NINA is the perfect venue for group dining, and we can accommodate large parties with prior notice.

Are gift cards available?

Gift cards are available for purchase on our website and in-store, making them a perfect gift for any occasion.
